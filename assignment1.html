Assignment 1: Blink!

My Blink Circuit!

The assignment: A circuit that limits the current flowing through at least 3 LEDs using resistors.

For my circuit, I decided to use Red, White, and Blue LEDs. In order of flashing, I used Pin 11 to power the Blue LED, Pin 12 for the White LED, Pin 13 for the Red LED, and connected GND to the negative side of the breadboard. I calculated the resistance for each LED with respect to their drop voltage (Red = 1.8V, Blue & White = 3.3V) and found that each calculation was within 1k Ohms, so I used 1k Ohm resistors for each LED.
