Final Project Idea!

Beat the Heat: Protecting those in the Back Seat

An idea I had was to create a temperature detector for users who leave their pets or children in enclosed spaces that are subject to overheating, like a car, covered strollers, or poorly insulated rooms in a house. What immediately comes to mind is using Arduino's Thermistor and possibly LEDs to generate some type of feedback as to whether the enclosed space is too hot to keep an animal or small child in.
